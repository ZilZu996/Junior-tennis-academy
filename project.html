JUNIOR TENNIS ACADEMY Purpose

Our junior tennis programs help kids learn in an environment that focuses on camaraderie, self-esteem, physical fitness, discipline, and good sportsmanship. They will develop just as many life skills as they will tennis skills.

PHILOSOPHY AND VALUES

At the Junior Tennis Academy, we prepare players for the future of tennis, applying innovative training systems combined with the characteristics that are synonymous with Rafa and his will to win:

  • Athleticism
  • Winning spirit
  • Competitive mentality
  • Intensity in training & during matches
  • Concentration
  • Anticipation
  • Tactical knowledge
  • Learning to compete

COMPETITION CAMP

PUT YOUR GAME TO THE TEST WITH A COMPETITION CAMP!

FOR AGES 11 AND OVER

This ultra-intensive, 15-day training camp with at least two tournaments is aimed at players looking to experience competitive tennis or push themselves with high-level matches.

DEDICATED TRAINING TIME

• Tennis: 3hrs/day
• Physical fitness and team sports: 3 hrs/day
• Official tournament matches (subject to invitation)
• 1 Tournament within the 2 Weeks Camps period.

Lunch included

BOARDING (ages 10 and over): arrival on Sunday, training starts Monday
DAY STUDENTS: training Monday to Saturday
